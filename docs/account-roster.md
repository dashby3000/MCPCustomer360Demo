# Account Roster

This synthetic portfolio is designed so the same account looks meaningfully different across systems, while still telling one coherent story.

## Archetypes

- Healthy expansion customers: `Alturas Health`, `Northstar Retail Group`, `Meridian BioLabs`, `Polaris Telecom`, `Oakwell Capital`
- Healthy but demanding strategic customers: `BluePeak Logistics`, `IronCore Manufacturing`, `Nimbus Insurance`
- Watch-list accounts: `Solstice Media`, `Lumen Energy Services`
- High-risk renewal accounts: `Redwood Bank`, `Harbor Hotels`, `Cedar Public Schools`, `Vanta Freight`
- Support-heavy but loyal account: `FreshFork Market`

## Explicit churn-risk anchors

- `Redwood Bank`
  - Renewal pressure, sponsor change, declining usage, repeated compliance and permissions complaints
- `Harbor Hotels`
  - Mobile friction, champion loss, weak adoption, high support load
- `Cedar Public Schools`
  - Weak rollout, reporting and permissions blockers, reactive CS coverage
- `Vanta Freight`
  - Integration pain appears in calls, support, and product feedback

## Best-customer anchors

- `Alturas Health`
  - Broad adoption, strong executive sponsorship, visible ROI
- `Oakwell Capital`
  - High-value executive use case and strong reporting adoption
- `Meridian BioLabs`
  - Healthy rollout with a focused compliance ask
- `Northstar Retail Group`
  - Strong multi-team adoption with expansion headroom

## Roadmap themes

- `rbac_permissions`
  - Especially painful for `Redwood Bank`, `Harbor Hotels`, and `Cedar Public Schools`
- `reporting_exports`
  - Shows up in executive-review and board-reporting workflows
- `audit_logs`
  - Driven by regulated customers
- `workflow_automation`
  - Expansion lever for operationally mature customers, blocker for weaker rollouts
- `integrations`
  - Most visible in logistics, manufacturing, telecom, and energy
- `mobile_experience`
  - Frontline manager pain, especially in hospitality, retail, and field operations
