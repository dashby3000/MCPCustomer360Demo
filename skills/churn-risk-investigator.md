# Churn Risk Investigator

Use this skill when you want to identify which accounts deserve attention before renewal.

## Recommended source order

1. Salesforce for renewal timing, ARR, and ownership
2. Gainsight for health, CTAs, sponsor changes, and success plans
3. Zendesk for support burden and escalation tone
4. Usage for adoption trend confirmation
5. Gong for direct customer language and objection themes

## Output shape

- Ranked risk list
- Short reason summary per account
- Evidence grouped by source
- Suggested next action for the account team

## Good questions

- Which accounts are most likely to churn?
- Which upcoming renewals need executive attention?
- Why is this account's health declining?
