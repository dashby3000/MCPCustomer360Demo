# Customer Pattern Finder

Use this skill when you want to compare strong and weak customers and extract repeatable patterns.

## Recommended source order

1. Usage for breadth and direction of adoption
2. Gainsight for health and sponsor stability
3. Salesforce for segment and commercial context
4. Zendesk for support intensity
5. Gong for qualitative nuance

## Output shape

- Shared traits among best customers
- Shared failure patterns among weak customers
- Contrasts between support-heavy loyal accounts and true churn risks
- Practical recommendations for sales and success teams

## Good questions

- What do our best customers have in common?
- Which support-heavy accounts are still healthy?
- What separates strong enterprise accounts from weak ones?
