# Roadmap Builder

Use this skill when you want to turn customer pain and feature demand into a draft roadmap.

## Recommended source order

1. Productboard for request volume and themes
2. Salesforce for ARR and renewal stakes
3. Gainsight for churn and health urgency
4. Zendesk for frequency of support friction
5. Gong for direct customer language

## Output shape

- Top themes by frequency and business impact
- Which themes are churn blockers versus expansion enablers
- A 3 to 5 item roadmap proposal
- Suggested messaging for customers waiting on each theme

## Good questions

- What features do customers request most often?
- Which product gaps are tied to churn risk?
- Build a roadmap from the voice of the customer
